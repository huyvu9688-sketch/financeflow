What is Personal Budget?

A personal budget is a financial plan that helps you manage your income and expenses. By allocating specific amounts to different spending categories like housing, food, and entertainment, you can ensure you're living within your means while saving for future goals.

Our smart budgeting tool tracks your spending in real-time, alerts you when you're approaching limits, and provides visual insights to help you make better financial decisions every day.
